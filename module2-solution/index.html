About IE

The practice of hatha yoga, as we commonly know it, is but one of eight branches of body of knowledge that is yoga. In fact, yoga is sophisticated system of self-empowerment that is capable of harnessing and activating inner energies in such a way that you body and mind function at their optimal capacity. It is means to create inner situations exactly the way you want them, turning you into the architet of your own joy.
